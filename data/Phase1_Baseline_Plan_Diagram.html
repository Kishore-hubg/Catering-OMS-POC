🍽️ Nidhi Catering – How Phase 1 Works

A plain-English look at what changes and what stays exactly the same once Phase 1 is live.

✅ What Stays Exactly the Same

Your ordering style doesn't change at all.

  • Customers still browse your website and call you directly
  • You still chat, upsell, and build the order over the phone
  • Rough notes on paper — still perfectly fine
  • You still create your own Excel estimate the same way you do today
  • You still send the estimate to the customer over WhatsApp / Email for their OK
